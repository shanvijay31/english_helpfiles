###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetCurSel

###Description###
Retrieve the index of the currently selected item

###Syntax###
#include <GuiComboBox.au3>
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetCurSel($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Zero-based index of the currently selected item
Failure: -1

###Remarks###
None.
